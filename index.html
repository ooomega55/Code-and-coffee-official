About the Club

Coding and Coffee is a club for the Web Design Pathway Program (WDPP). The main goal of the club is to help each other with coding issues and give advice. The club advisors are Mr. Kilgore and Mrs. Leonard. The WDPP Advisory Board members act as tutors for all the members in the program. Anyone can become a tutor though for volunteer hours.
